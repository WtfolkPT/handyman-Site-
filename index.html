Silver Coast Homeowner Tips

💧 Combatting Humidity

Check your bathroom silicone every 12 months. Fresh seals prevent hidden mold behind tiles—crucial for coastal living.

🧱 Solid Wall Mounting

Portuguese walls aren't hollow. Hanging a heavy TV safely requires masonry anchors, not just standard screws. I always carry professional-grade hardware.

🛡️ Smart Security Tips

Video doorbells (Ring/Eufy) are great, but solar-powered versions work best in our sunny climate to avoid manual battery charging every month.

📦 Wardrobe Stabilization

IKEA PAX wardrobes are top-heavy. Always anchor them to the wall. It’s a 10-minute job that ensures safety for the whole family.
