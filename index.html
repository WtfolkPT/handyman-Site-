Silver Coast Homeowner Tips

💧 Combatting Humidity

Living near the ocean (Foz, Peniche, Nazaré) means moisture. Tip: Check your bathroom silicone every 12 months to prevent hidden mold growth behind tiles.

🧱 Drilling Into Stone

Portuguese walls aren't drywall—they are often solid brick or stone. To hang a heavy TV, you need specific wall plugs. I always use professional-grade anchors for peace of mind.

📶 The "Stone Wall" Wi-Fi Fix

Traditional routers struggle with thick local walls. A 'Mesh' system is usually the best solution for full-house coverage without running wires.

📦 IKEA Assembly Tips

Save your floor! Always assemble on the cardboard box your furniture came in to avoid scratching your tiles or the wood finish.
